There is a house at the end of a road that was not there yesterday. It was built out
of grief, the way some houses are built out of brick, and it has been waiting a long
time for someone to come home.

ENCORE is a horror choose-your-path gamebook anthology. Every season is a different
nightmare; the same impresario runs the show. Season One is **The Haunt**. The doors
open soon.
