There is a house at the end of a road that was not there yesterday. It was built out
of grief, the way some houses are built out of brick, and it has been waiting a long
time for someone to come home.

ENCORE is a choose-your-path horror anthology. Every book is a different nightmare;
the same impresario, the Curator, runs the show, and the same company of the dead is
recast into every production, recognisable only by the tells they cannot help.

Book One is **The Haunt**. You wake in a cold parlour with no memory of dying, and a
card on the table says you have been cast. You decide where to step, what to pick up,
and what you are willing to set down, and the house remembers what you carry. The
catch the whole house is built around: the way out, the ending the Curator calls
winning, costs you the very thing you came to keep.
