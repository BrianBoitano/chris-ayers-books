Two things are cooking, so here is the status nobody asked for.

First: the **Signal to Noise** trilogy (Sarge's side of The Broadcast) is going into
audio. Yes, the funniest and most literal narrator in the universe is getting a voice.
No, he does not think this is a good use of anyone's time, and he has prepared a
spreadsheet explaining why. The books are live now; the audiobooks are in production
and will land here when they are done.

Second, and this is the new one: I am building a brand-new world called **ENCORE**.
It is not The Broadcast. It is horror, it is a choose-your-path gamebook, and every
season is a different nightmare with the same impresario running the show. Season One
is called **The Haunt**.

Here is your teaser, and that is all you get:

> There is a house at the end of a road that was not there yesterday. It was built
> out of grief, the way some houses are built out of brick, and it has been waiting
> a long time for someone to come home. The Curator will see you now. He always keeps
> the seat warm. He is so very glad you decided to play.
>
> Every door is a choice. Some of them are doors out.

More when the lights are right.
