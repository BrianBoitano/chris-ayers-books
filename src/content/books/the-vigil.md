You came back for someone the house will not let go. The Vigil opens in a warm place, lit and perfumed, an endless party that is also an endless wake, where nobody is allowed to die and nobody is allowed to leave. Somewhere in it is a person you knew, kept like a candle that is never permitted to gutter, and the coven has set a chair for you beside them.

The Vigil is Book Two of ENCORE, a choose-your-path horror. The same Curator runs the show; the same company of the dead is recast into a new production. Bring the character you built in The Haunt, and the house remembers what you carried, who you are, and whether you have ever once let go.

You read by deciding. Take the cup or refuse it. Speak the name or hold your tongue. Your choices decide which of the book's endings the house gives you, and the way out, the true one, asks the hardest question the series has yet asked: whether love can let the beloved finally end.
